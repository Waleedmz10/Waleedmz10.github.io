Are Dashcams Secure? A Security Perspective
Introduction

Dashcams have become increasingly popular worldwide, not only for recording road accidents but also for enhancing driver accountability and personal security. As vehicles become more connected and integrated with smart technologies, dashcams are evolving into IoT devices with advanced features such as Wi-Fi connectivity, cloud storage, GPS tracking, and mobile app integration.

However, this raises an important cybersecurity question:

Are dashcams secure? Or are they just another vulnerable IoT device on wheels?
